Project Overview

BeTalent needed a robust e-commerce platform to showcase and sell their products online. The requirement was a full-featured webshop that could handle complex inventory management, multiple payment options, and provide an exceptional shopping experience.

We built a comprehensive solution featuring a modern webshop frontend, powerful inventory management system, seamless payment integration, and detailed product management capabilities that allow the client to easily manage their entire catalog.
