The Challenge

Our client, a leader in international freight, faced a fragmented digital landscape. Legacy systems were isolated, leading to manual data entry errors, delayed tracking updates, and a lack of real-time visibility across the supply chain.

The objective was clear: consolidate global operations into a single, cohesive platform that leverages machine learning to predict delays and automate routine scheduling tasks, reducing the human overhead in complex logistics routing.

Real-time fleet monitoring and predictive analytics interface.

Technical Architecture

We developed a cloud-native architecture using React & Next.js for a responsive front-end, supported by a Python-based AI engine. This engine processes historical shipping data to optimize routes in real-time.
